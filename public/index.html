Scotty's Simple Weather API

Real-time weather data and alerts with automatic 5-minute updates.

  • Receive current weather conditions including temperature, humidity, and wind speed.
  • Active weather alerts and advisories from the National Weather Service are also included.
  • The Data caches for 5 minutes to avoid hitting OpenWeather's 1000 calls/day rate limit.
